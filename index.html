Our internship program is three years old! And we’re making it very practical this year. We’re running a challenge for each of our departments: Design (Graphic, UI/UX), Website Design, Web Development (Frontend & Backend), and Mobile Development (Android/iOS).

We like to work with students who are driven, proactive and have made progress in their interest area. You should have gained some working experience, not on school projects per se, but live projects. Maybe you built an app, some graphic designs, or UI for your mom’s store, lecturer’s business, your own business, etc.

We don’t just want passion, we want students who have work experience and still have a lot to learn.

Choose your interest and get started!
